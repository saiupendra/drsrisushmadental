General Dentistry Services

Comprehensive dental care for healthy smiles - Check-ups, cleaning, and preventive treatments

What is General Dentistry?

General Dentistry is the foundation of oral health, focusing on prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of common dental conditions.

Our General Dentistry Services

  • Dental Check-ups & Consultations - Regular oral health assessments
  • Professional Teeth Cleaning - Plaque and tartar removal
  • Cavity Fillings - Tooth-colored restorations
  • Gum Disease Treatment - Periodontal care and management
  • Fluoride Treatment - Strengthening tooth enamel
  • Dental Sealants - Protection for cavity-prone areas
  • X-rays & Digital Imaging - Advanced diagnostics

Benefits of Regular Dental Visits

Regular check-ups help detect issues early, prevent serious problems, and keep your smile bright. With 10+ years of expertise, Dr Sri Sushma provides painless, modern general dentistry for patients of all ages in Hyderabad.
