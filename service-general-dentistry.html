Top Tips for Oral Health & General Dentistry

Brush Twice, Every Day

Use a soft-bristle brush and fluoride toothpaste to gently clean teeth and gums for two minutes, morning and night.

Floss Daily

Clean between your teeth with floss or a water flosser every day to remove plaque from places your brush can't reach.

Limit Sugary and Acidic Foods

Reduce fruit juices, fizzy drinks, and sticky snacks to protect your enamel and prevent cavities.

Visit Your Dentist Every 6 Months

Regular checkups keep your mouth healthy and pain-free by catching potential problems early.

Avoid Tobacco

Smoking and chewing tobacco are leading causes of gum disease and oral cancer—quit for a brighter, safer smile.

Drink Plenty of Water

Water helps rinse away food acids and bacteria, keeping your teeth cleaner between brushes.
