Oral Surgery

Safe, professional oral surgical treatments for dental health and comfort

Expert Oral Surgical Care

Oral surgery addresses complex dental issues requiring surgical intervention. Our experienced team performs procedures safely and comfortably using modern techniques and anesthesia.

Oral Surgical Procedures

  • Wisdom Teeth Extractions - Safe removal of impacted wisdom teeth
  • Surgical Tooth Extractions - Complex tooth removal
  • Dental Implant Placement - Surgical tooth replacement
  • Frenectomy - Tongue or lip tie correction
  • Cyst/Tumor Removal - Oral lesion excision
  • Bone Grafting - Jaw bone regeneration
  • Pre-prosthetic Surgery - Preparing for dentures/implants

Your Comfort is Our Priority

All surgical procedures are performed in sterile conditions with advanced equipment. We provide comprehensive post-operative care instructions and follow-up to ensure smooth recovery.
