Orthodontics

Braces and clear aligners to straighten teeth and boost confidence at any age

Straighten Your Smile

Orthodontics corrects misaligned teeth and jaws, improving both appearance and oral health. Whether you're a teen or adult, it's never too late for a straighter, healthier smile.

Orthodontic Treatment Options

  • Metal Braces - Traditional, effective tooth alignment
  • Ceramic Braces - Tooth-colored, less visible option
  • Clear Aligners (Invisalign-style) - Removable, nearly invisible
  • Lingual Braces - Hidden behind teeth
  • Palatal Expanders - Create space in the jaw
  • Retainers - Maintain results after treatment
  • Space Maintainers - For children losing baby teeth

Benefits of Orthodontic Treatment

Proper alignment improves bite, speech, oral health, and confidence! We use digital scans and advanced technology for precise, comfortable fitting and faster results.
